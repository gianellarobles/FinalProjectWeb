Purpose:

 This website is designed to create a welcoming and healthy environment for good habits.

 As we get older, it becomes more difficult to lose weight or feel confident with our bodies. Some common factors that can lead to weight gain are:

 
  1. Poor diet
  2. Lack of exercise
  3. Genetics
  4. Stress
  5. Environmental factor
 

The reason I started this website was to healthy and smart ways to lose weight. Sometimes it can get difficult and frustrating not seeing results quickly, but the goal is create a plan that is right for you. The best way to start is to plan excerises ahead of time. Additionally, tracking your excerises can demonsrate your progess in the long run.
